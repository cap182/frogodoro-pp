Privacy Policy for Frogodoro Chrome Extension

Last Updated: July 2, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Frogodoro does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from users. The extension operates entirely locally within your browser.

Local Data Storage:

  • Timer Settings: Your custom work/break durations and session preferences
  • Progress Data: Number of completed Pomodoro sessions and daily progress
  • Skin Preferences: Your selected visual themes and customization choices
  • Extension Settings: Any personalization options you configure

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API and is never transmitted to external servers.

2. How We Use Information

The locally stored data is used solely to:

  • Maintain your personal timer preferences between browser sessions
  • Track your productivity progress for motivational purposes
  • Remember your skin selections and customization choices
  • Provide a personalized user experience

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not:

  • Share, sell, or transfer any user data to third parties
  • Use data for advertising or marketing purposes
  • Collect analytics or tracking information
  • Access your browsing history or website content
  • Store personal identification information

4. Permissions Required

Storage Permission:

Used to save your timer settings, progress, and preferences locally on your device.

Alarms Permission:

Used to schedule timer notifications for work/break transitions, even when the browser is minimized.

5. Data Security

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • We do not have access to your stored information
  • Data is automatically deleted if you uninstall the extension

6. Children's Privacy

Frogodoro does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The extension is designed for general productivity use.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

8. Contact Information

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store listing.

9. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, modify, or delete. All locally stored data can be cleared by uninstalling the extension.

This privacy policy confirms that Frogodoro respects your privacy and operates as a local productivity tool without any data collection or sharing.
